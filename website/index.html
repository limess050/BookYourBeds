What is BookYourBeds?

BookYourBeds is a simple and cost effective online booking system, which helps to increase your business. By clearly providing your room or bed availability, potential guests can book without any need for further enquiry. Read more...

BookYourBeds has been designed around the principles of simplicity and practicality. This applies to both office management and guest booking experience.

Why adopt BookYourBeds?

BookYourBeds helps convert your site visitors into your guests. By showing availability and allowing potential customers to book immediately, you increase the likelihood of converting website visitors into paying guests! Read more...

Today’s online customers want instant booking satisfaction. They don’t want to have to wait for responses to emails or enquiries, or have to spend time contacting you by telephone. By adopting BookYourBeds you will give your customers the service they want.

It has been predicted by Google that mobile internet browsing through devices such as Smart Phones will surpass traditional browsing methods later in 2013. Some hostel providers have recorded that up to 80% of their bookings are now coming through mobile devices. BookYourBeds has been designed to be fully mobile compatible; this benefit is included in your monthly price! With mobile functionality, BookYourBeds has brought the future of tourism into the present!

How do I get BookYourBeds?

Just click on the click here, and follow the instructions in the set up wizard! Read more...

At the end of the sign up process you will be given your unique BookYourBeds.com web address also referred to as a URL. If you have a web designer who created your website for you, ask them to create a link on your website such as a ‘book now’ button and link it with your unique URL provided, or replace an ‘enquire’ button to link to your new URL rather than an existing enquiry form. It’s quick, and easy!

If you have designed or are designing your website yourself using an online website builder, there should be instructions on how to add a link(s), on the website builder’s own website. If not, contact them directly to find out how to do this.
